240 /*
241 This stores the most recently selected day.
242 It is useful when considering the following scenario:
243
244 1. Date is: 31/01/2000
245 2. User increments month: 29/02/2000
246 3. User increments month: 31/03/2000
247
248 At step 1, cachedDay stores 31. At step 2, the 31 is invalid for February, so the cachedDay is not updated.
249 At step 3, the month is changed to March, for which 31 is a valid day. Since 29 < 31, the day is set to cachedDay.
250 This is good for when users have selected their desired day and are scrolling up or down in the month or year section
251 and do not want smaller months (or non-leap years) to alter the day that they chose.
252 */
253 mutable int cachedDay = -1;
